What is EARTHSwap.Finance?

EARTHSwap.Finance is a complete on-chain instant swap protocol that operates with an on-chain liquidity pool, where liquidity is guaranteed by automated market-making (AMM) mechanisms. It is an on-chain swap service that allows anyone that has any EARTH or ECT-type token cryptocurrency to become a liquidity provider and earn income from transaction fee commissions. In EARTHswap, Ethereum-based tokens (ETH, UNI, DAI, WBTC) can be transferred to the EARTHSwap.Finance ecosystem to offer yield farming with asset pairings previously unconnected in the decentralized world.

The ESF token is the governance token of the EARTHswap platform.

Holders have the responsibility and authority to determine governance agendas in compliance with the protocol related rules and regulations to lead the development of the service. As such, ESF holders directly participate in deciding things like fees, mining distribution, and contracts. Another main utility of ESF is for creating new liquidity pools. A specific amount of ESF must be paid as a pool contract creation fee when a new ECT token pair pool is created on EARTHswap.
