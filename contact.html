What is Shelf?

Shelf brings all your email newsletters to one place, far away from your primary inbox.

subscribe to newsletters with a custom you@shelf.news email address, or automatically forward them from your existing address.

visit your Shelf on the web or the app to read your newsletters in a beautiful layout, on your own time.

keep your inbox clean and stay engaged with your favorite content.
